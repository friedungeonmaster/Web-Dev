Tunnel Vision

Posted on 1 February at 2:55 AM

Focus. It is very important to achieve your goals. When you spray and water everything at once, then this "everything at once" grows slower than if you watered one thing. Focus on your goal and don't let anything get in the way of that focus. And it can be knocked down by various factors, for example:

  • Toxic relationship
  • Friends (drinking buddies)
  • Problems with the society in which you constantly live (university, work)
  • And many more different things

It's always people's fault. To teach yourself not to lose focus, you need to learn not to lose your head from people. A cool head is the key to success. Prioritize. "These miracles are temporary but your name is forever"
